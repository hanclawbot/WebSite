提示詞結構指南

六大核心要素

  • 風格標籤 (30%) — 核心流派如 Pop, Rock, EDM
  • 情緒氛圍 (20%) — upbeat, melancholic, romantic
  • 樂器配置 (20%) — piano, strings, synth bass
  • 節奏速度 (15%) — 120 bpm, syncopated, groovy
  • 人聲特質 (10%) — female vocals, falsetto
  • 混音效果 (5%) — reverb-heavy, clean production
